catacaraga

This little event website is a (tame) tribute to one of the greatest writers of Latin America, Gabriel Garcia Marquez. I spent a lot of time thinking about the design and the background story and when I found myself spending much more time on the background I told myself: 'Enough mate. Do the design and if you really want to develop a story, do it in your spare time.'
